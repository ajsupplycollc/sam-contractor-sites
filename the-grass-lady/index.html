South Florida's Lawn Problem, Solved

Chinch bugs. Water bills. Weekend mowing in 95-degree heat. There's a better way.

South Florida water isn't cheap. Artificial turf eliminates irrigation completely. Your lawn stays green while your neighbors' sprinklers run all night.

UV-stabilized fibers that won't fade or break down under Florida sun. Looks as good in year 10 as the day it was installed.
